CS FAQ


Please consult this FAQ before e-mailing anyone on the team. Questions have been answered by both Gooseman and CLIFFE. If you have read this FAQ and still have a question, comment, or concern, feel free to e-mail CLIFFE.

Misc.issues :
Can I be a beta tester for the next release?
Generally, no. We have a testing team that we trust assembled. It's mostly people who are friends of ours. Random people probably won't be selected.

What's with this "yogurt cannon" weapon, is it for real?
Yes, it will be included in the next beta and will launch raunchy loads of creme.

When will beta x.x be released!?
E-mailing us this question will not get you any more information than you can find on the main page. If it isn't posted.. we don't know. In fact, sometimes we don't know until hours before the release. If we know, we'll post it.

What's with this trout slapping thing?
It originates in irc, it's an easy command to slap someone (like rze).

Your get hosted page has been taken down, are you hosting any more sites?
We are pretty much through with hosting sites, we feel the C-S.net "network" is sufficient and strong. However, by invitation we may ask certain sites if they are interested in hosting.

Will you slap me?
You could be next.

Why did you move away from Planethalflife/Gamespy?
We wanted to get our own domain and be able to host sites. PHL doesn't allow that. We left. Simple. It's not because they censored our interview during mod of the week (though that was slightly maddening).

WTF! I got telefragged in a game, what's with that?
Counter-Strike only supports up to 20 players. Each map only has 20 spawn points. If a server admin runs a server with more than 20 players, there will be telefragging.

Why did you choose to limit it to 20 players?
Any more than 20 players turns into chaos and your framerate drops. That's no fun.

Who is MTG?
A young kid who thoroughly spammed the CS forums back when we were hosted @ PHL.

If I e-mail you suggestions, will you read them/use them?
We will read them but we can't promise to use them. Suggestion e-mails will generally not get a reply, there's just too many sent in.

If I send you CS news, will you post it?
Probably not. We want to keep the main c-s.net updates strictly about mod development progress. All general news should be sent into and will be posted @ CSnation. Contact Rze.

Who is Gooseman, where have I seen your name before?
I've worked on a few mods, Navy SEALS for Quake1 and Action Quake2. My role in Navy SEALS involved every aspect of the mod except for mapping and tech-fucking-support. Getting back to coding is a welcome (I hope) change since it allows me to have much more control over the mod content.

Do you need help with the web site? I make super l33t graphicz!!
No. We have it covered.

I e-mailed you and you didn't respond... WTF?
Please remember that we get almost 100 e-mails per day and cannot possibly reply to them all. All e-mails are read however.

Will you link to my clan/CS related site?
No. Keeping updated links is cumbersome. We only post links of the utmost importance. We will not pimp your site in our news, as it is strictly for mod news.

I'm having technical problems.. can you help me?
No. We do not have time to be tech support. Ask on the CS forums for help.

I want a [insert weapon name here] in the mod, will you include it?
We will probably not honor weapon requests.

Do you need any help with the Mod?
The one thing we need help with is mappers, all your mapping experts head over to the mapping specs page and help us out!

Can I send you a Pic Of the Day?
Please do not.

Why aren't you putting in hyperblasters and the like into the Mod?
A hyperblaster is just a slower version of a pistol but with different graphics. If hyperblasters are your cup of tea then I suggest you wait for a SCI-Fi mod or better yet, Quake3.

Why did you leave the Action Quake2/Action Half-Life team?
Gooseman: I wasn't entirely motivated to make models geared to the Action them anymore. My models never really fit to well in Action Quake2 as it was. Leaving the A-Team was a hard decision and I still keep in touch with the members. They're a funny bunch of guys with a sense of humour not unlike my own. I just wish they were into military shit more. Hopefully though, our mods won't compete for a fan base since our mods have different enough themes. It's hard enough trying to get a fan base with all the other military style mods and games out there, I'd rather not have to compete with AHL and it's huge fan base.

Where can I download the f'n mod?
See the downloads page for all that you'll need.

Where can I see screenshots of models and/or in-game shots?
All model and misc shots can be found at the weapons page, while all in-game screenshots can be found at the images page.

Gooseman, can you help me with modeling? How about mapping?
I'm too pressed for time to be giving lessons to be people who want to learn how to model. You're best bet is to visit www.planetquake.com/q2pmp You can find tutorials there and some models that would put mine to shame. Now scat ya lousy begger!
As for mapping, unless you want to know how to make Doom2 maps you're best bet is to ask someone else. I've never made maps for Quake2 or Half-Life.

What is your favorite Backstreet Boys song?
Everybooddyyy.. yeaahhh! Rock yerrr boooddyyy!! Yeahhhh!

Why wasn't the 747 map included in beta3/4?
Leon's 747 map is still a work in progress and may make beta5. Ultimately, we don't know the exact status of it.

I can't buy ammo for the Desert Eagle and Shotgun! WTF?!
Type "bind 6 slot6" and "bind 7 slot7" at the console. This will now work. You also want to repeat this process for 8 and 9.

Why the delay on the linux port?
The linux port will take time after each release to be ported, tested, and released. Please have patience on it. We'll post news on how it's coming along.

My ammo is screwed up. It says I have no more ammo, but I reload and more appears! Will this be fixed?
This is a bug that is being worked on.

I am a weapons expert and you did gun x all wrong, here's how you do it..!
Thanks. I like to play in the ball pit.

If I get killed while in a camera, I can't get out! Will this be fixed?
This is not a top priority, though we are looking into it.

The money system is messed up! I think it should be this way... will you fix it?
The money system will continue to be tweaked.

Why the F%#@ are your guns left handed?
Gooseman: I animate better when the models are on the left hand side therefore the models will end up being left handed unless I figure out an easy way to mirror them. And no, it's not a simple matter of just mirroring the 3d mesh.. There's a standard bones system that I have to worry about as well!

Does Counter-Strike include Single Player? How about Deathmatch?
Just teamplay, multiplayer. CS does not support single player nor will it in the future.

Map.issues :
Can I still submit a map for Counter-Strike although betas are already out?
Yes! We will continue to add new maps as the mod is developed and more releases are made. Head on over to the mapping information page for more stuff.

Will there be a site for Counter-Strike maps?
No. From my experiences of working for the Action Quake2 Map Depot, having one only makes the map situation for a mod a complete and utter mess. We are asking that *no one* start a map site for the mod. To get fresh maps out there, we are going to continue to accept new maps and they will be included in new releases of the mod. This will be a boon for everyone! It will make the map situation *much* more oganized and save people *a lot* of headaches.

I'm going to make a maps site, do you mind?
We would prefer if you would not make a CS maps site. It would turn the map situation into confusion. This is for everyone's benefit and will make the mod much more enjoyable for everyone. New maps will come with new releases. Of course we can't "control" the map situation, we just hope you will understand our reasoning for not setting up an official maps site. People will do as they choose, but just remember how many megs of maps you have to download if you want to play AQ2!!

Will you release a mappack then?
Probably.

Where can I get info/screenshots of the included maps?
Head on over to the CS Maps page.
